Student mobility, redesigned

Find people near you who are leaving for the same place.

Trippy helps students coordinate safe, social, flexible trips home. Match with friends, classmates, or trusted new people, then move into a private room to chat and choose the best way to get there.

The flow

A cleaner way to leave with people you trust.

01

Signal when you want to leave

Set your destination zone, departure time, and who can see you: friends, your school, or everyone.

02

Filter for trust and fit

Refine matches by year, gender preference, vibe, travel mode, and social context before committing.

03

Coordinate the trip

Open a private room, compare Uber, Lyft, bus, or walk options, and make the trip together.
