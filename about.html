TRIPPY

Opportunity and context

Students often leave the same place at the same time, but coordinate that travel badly.

End-of-day travel is often improvised through group chats, texts, or walking alone. The gap is not just transportation. It is trust, timing, visibility, and confidence in who you are joining.

Problem

Unsafe and inefficient departure behavior

Students frequently make last-minute travel decisions after classes, events, or nights out. Existing tools do not help them discover nearby peers with the same route and departure window.

Context

Travel decisions are social decisions

The decision to share a ride or walk is shaped by familiarity, school affiliation, comfort level, and identity cues, not just route optimization.

Solution

An information system for matching and coordination

Trippy turns fragmented departure behavior into one flow: signal intent, filter by trust, discover peers, and coordinate transport through a shared room.

Visual system concept

The product interface makes trust visible before transit starts.

The solution is intentionally built around verified school identity, visible Instagram handles, preference filters, and controlled audience settings so students can decide who feels appropriate to travel with.
